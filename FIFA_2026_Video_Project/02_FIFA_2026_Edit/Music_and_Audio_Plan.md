## Levels (CapCut)
- Music: −6 dB during VO sections, full (0 dB) in no-VO sections (0:21–0:27, 0:39–0:45).
- VO: 0 dB, sits on top. Enable CapCut **"Auto-duck"** so music dips under the voice.
- SFX: −3 to −8 dB, accents only.
- Master target: **−14 LUFS** for social platforms (don't peak above −1 dB).
