## Transition cheat-sheet (CapCut names)
- **Hard cut** — no transition, just butt clips together.
- **Whip pan** — CapCut → Transitions → *"Swirl"* or *"Whip"* (~0.3s).
- **Match cut** — align motion/shape across the cut (ball → city dome etc.); no effect needed.
- **Zoom-in** — Transitions → *"Zoom in"* (0.4s).
- **Cross-dissolve** — Transitions → *"Dissolve"* (0.4s).
- **Flash-frame** — insert 1-frame white between clips, or Transitions → *"Flash white"*.
- **Speed ramp** — select clip → Speed → *Curve* → "Bullet"/"Hero" preset.
- **Freeze-frame** — select clip → *Freeze* at the last frame, extend 1.5s.

## Pacing logic
- 0:00–0:12 — establish, ~2-3s holds. Let it breathe.
- 0:12–0:21 — speeding up, location story.
- 0:21–0:33 — mid-tempo emotional beat.
- 0:33–0:45 — climax: 0.5s machine-gun cuts → freeze on logo at the drop.
