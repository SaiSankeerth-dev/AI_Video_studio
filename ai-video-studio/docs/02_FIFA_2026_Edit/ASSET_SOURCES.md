# FIFA 2026 Hype — Legally Usable Asset Sources

**Read first:** You cannot legally use FIFA broadcast footage, match clips, official logos, or
the World Cup trophy/emblem. Those are trademarked and rights-protected. This list uses **only**
royalty-free / CC0 / Creative Commons / public-domain sources that are free for commercial use.
For each, download the best match into the matching slot folder/name in `03_Assets/Video_Clips/`.

> When you download, log it in `ASSET_LICENCES.md` (template at bottom) so QA can verify clearance.

## Free video libraries (all free for commercial use)
- **Pexels Video** — https://www.pexels.com/videos/ — CC0-style Pexels licence, no attribution.
- **Pixabay Video** — https://pixabay.com/videos/ — Pixabay licence, no attribution.
- **Mixkit** — https://mixkit.co/free-stock-video/ — free Mixkit licence.
- **Coverr** — https://coverr.co/ — free for commercial use.
- **Videvo (free tier)** — https://www.videvo.net/ — check per-clip licence label.
